Taj Mahal

Location: Agra, Uttar Pradesh • Best time: Oct - Mar

A world-famous ivory-white marble mausoleum built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most iconic symbols of India.

Learn more

Jaipur (Pink City)

Location: Rajasthan • Best time: Oct - Mar

Capital of Rajasthan known for its historic palaces, forts (Amber Fort, Nahargarh), colorful bazaars and rich Rajasthani culture and cuisine.

Learn more

Varanasi

Location: Uttar Pradesh • Best time: Sep - Mar

One of the world's oldest living cities on the banks of the Ganges. Known for ghats, spiritual rituals, ancient temples and vibrant cultural life.

Learn more

Goa

Location: West India • Best time: Nov - Feb

Famous for sandy beaches, Portuguese-influenced architecture, relaxed nightlife and seafood. Popular with beach lovers and water-sport enthusiasts.

Learn more

Kerala (Backwaters & Hill Stations)

Location: South India • Best time: Sep - Mar

Known for tranquil backwaters (Alleppey), houseboat stays, spice gardens and hill stations like Munnar. Often called "God's Own Country".

Learn more

Ladakh

Location: Northern India (Jammu & Kashmir region) • Best time: May - Sep

High-altitude desert landscapes, dramatic mountain passes, monasteries (e.g., Hemis), and crystal-clear lakes (Pangong Tso). Ideal for trekking and adventure travel.

Learn more

New Delhi

Location: National Capital Territory of Delhi • Best time: Oct - Mar

India's capital region with historic monuments (Qutub Minar, Red Fort), museums, bustling markets and a gateway to many parts of the country.

Learn more

Mumbai

Location: Maharashtra • Best time: Nov - Feb

India's financial capital: home to Bollywood, the Gateway of India, vibrant street food, colonial architecture and lively neighborhoods.

Learn more

Amritsar

Location: Punjab • Best time: Sep - Mar

Home to the Golden Temple (Harmandir Sahib), a central religious site for Sikhs, and famous for its Langar (community kitchen) and rich Punjabi culture.

Learn more

Hampi

Location: Karnataka • Best time: Oct - Feb

UNESCO World Heritage site with ancient ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire, massive boulders and unique landscape — perfect for history lovers and photographers.

Learn more

Tips for beginners

  • Check weather and local festivals before planning — seasons matter (monsoon vs winter).
  • Book popular attractions in advance (e.g., Taj Mahal tickets) to avoid queues.
  • Carry a printed or offline map for remote areas (Ladakh, backwaters).
  • Respect local customs and dress codes when visiting religious sites.
